Frequently Asked Questions

CineSphere operates on a subscription-based business model, offering members unlimited access to its extensive library of films for a monthly fee. This model allows customers to explore a diverse range of classic and contemporary movies without the constraints of individual rental costs. In addition to in-store rentals, CineSphere provides an online platform where subscribers can stream films directly to their devices, ensuring convenience and accessibility. By combining physical and digital offerings, CineSphere caters to both traditional movie enthusiasts and modern viewers, creating a seamless and flexible cinematic experience that meets the needs of its diverse clientele.

CineSphere is committed to ensuring customer satisfaction and offers a straightforward refund policy to address any concerns. If a customer encounters any issues with a rental, such as a damaged disc or streaming difficulties, they are encouraged to contact customer support within 48 hours for assistance. Refunds or exchanges will be provided for defective products or services that cannot be resolved. For subscription cancellations, customers can request a refund for the unused portion of their subscription within the first 14 days of the billing cycle. CineSphere strives to handle all refund requests promptly and fairly, ensuring a positive experience for all its patrons.
